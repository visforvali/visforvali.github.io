கண்ணகி அம்மன், or Kannaki Amman, is once a human woman, wife to a merchant who is unjustly killed. She confronts the king in a rage, proves her husband's innocence, and burns down the kingdom of Madurai for wronging her, after which she ascends. Like this, we reward feminine rage but remain terrorized by it. When Kannaki Amman seizes you, like a kind of பேய் பிட, pei pidichutu, when the spirit catches you, you let her.
